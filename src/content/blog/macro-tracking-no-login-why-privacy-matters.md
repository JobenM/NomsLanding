## Your Food Log Is More Personal Than You Think

What you eat reveals a lot about you. Dietary restrictions tied to religion or culture. Health conditions that influence food choices. Struggles with eating habits you'd rather keep private.

Most calorie tracking apps treat your food diary as data to be collected, analyzed, and monetized. Your meal logs are uploaded to their servers, associated with your account, and potentially shared with third parties.

NomsAI takes a fundamentally different approach: your food log never leaves your device.

## The Problem with Account-Based Health Apps

When a health app requires you to create an account, a few things happen:

1. **Your data becomes their asset.** Every meal you log, every macro you track, builds a profile that belongs to their servers — not you.
2. **You become a retention target.** Notifications, streaks, and gamification are engineered to keep you in the app, not to serve your health goals.
3. **A breach is possible.** Any data stored on external servers can be leaked. Health data breaches have real consequences.

## Device-First Design

NomsAI stores your food logs locally on your iPhone or Android. There is no account to create, no password to forget, no personal data sitting on a server waiting to be breached.

This means:
- **No sign-up friction.** Open the app and start tracking immediately.
- **No privacy risk.** Your meal history exists only on your device.
- **Works offline.** No internet connection required after the initial setup.
- **Delete any time.** Uninstall the app and your data is gone. No lingering records on anyone's servers.

## AI Without the Surveillance

You might wonder: if AI requires a server, how is this private?

When you log a meal, the description you type is sent to our servers to estimate your macros. That's it. The text is processed and immediately discarded — it's never stored in a database, never linked to your device ID, and never associated with any user profile. We have no record of what you ate.

## The Right Trade-off for Health Data

Privacy isn't just a feature — it's a philosophy. The health data you generate is yours. You should be able to track your nutrition without becoming a data point in someone else's product.

An AI food diary with no login requirement is not just more private — it's more trustworthy, faster to use, and respects you as a user rather than treating you as a data source.

That's the NomsAI promise.
